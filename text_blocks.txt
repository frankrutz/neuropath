textblock30001_de Die gemeinnützige Stiftung Neuropath nimmt auch Ihre Spende entgegen. Die Spenden werden getreu dem Stiftungszweck verwendet. textblock30001_en The non-profit foundation Neuropath also graciously accepts donations. Donations will be used in accordance with the foundation's aims and statutes. Please note that all donations to the Neuropath Foundation can be deducted from your income tax and we would be happy to provide you with a donation receipt. With your permission, we shall be happy to gratefully acknowledge your donation on our website as a way for us to thank you and recognize your contribution.
textblock30001_it La fondazione senza scopo di lucro Neuropath ringrazia per una vostra eventuale donazione. Le donazioni vengono strettamente utililizzate secondo gli statuti della fondazione. Su richiesta pubblichiamo la vostra donazione su questa pagina web.
