The Foundation of Neuropath is a non-profit foundation for the advancement of research within the field of Neuropathology, which explores various neurological diseases, among which include:

  • Creutzfeldt-Jakob
  • Alzheimer’s
  • Parkinson’s
  • Brain tumors
  • Multiple Sclerosis

It was founded in 2006 at the University of Zürich by Prof. Dr. Adriano Aguzzi.
