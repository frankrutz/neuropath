Foundation's Aims

The purpose of the foundation is to support neuropathological research at the University of Zurich. This support is provided through means of research and education, which is achievable due to the financing of the following activities and essential research components:

  • Scholarships for bachelor/master students, doctoral candidates, as well as postdocs and post-doctoral students
  • Acquisition of equipment to support research
  • Consumables for research
  • Publication costs
  • Research stays of the staff of the Institute of Neuropathology at external institutes
  • Research stays of foreign scientists at the Institute for Neuropathology of the University of Zurich
  • Organization and participation in scientific conferences
  • Scientific retreats of the staff at the Institute of Neuropathology
